About Me

Hi, my name is Kevin Cook, and I'm a recent graduate from Temple University with a degree in Communications - Media Studies & Production. Before coming to Temple, I attended Marist College in Poughkeepsie, NY, where I started in Advertising and ended up majoring in TV/Radio/Film. Right now I live right outside of Philadelphia, PA, and I edit a Netflix binge-watching web series called Binge & Purge for Scrapple TV. I have also been a production assistant for a few feature films, including American Exorcist and most recently Zeroes.

As a big fan of movies, TV, and video games, I try to find every opportunity to incorporate those interests into my work, which is why, at Marist, I started a television program called EXP: The Marist Gaming Experience that covered video game and geek news. Recently, my friend and I have been working on a comedy web series revolving around the popular pen & paper role-playing game Dungeons & Dragons, but that project is still currently in development.
